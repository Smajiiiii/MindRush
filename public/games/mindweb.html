MindWeb — Rules

Based on Asocijacije from the Serbian TV quiz show Slagalica.

Board: 4 columns (A–D), 4 cells each. Each column has a hidden answer. One FINAL answer connects all 4 columns.

4 states per turn:
1. Open a cell to reveal a word
2. Choose a column to guess (or Final)
3. Type your answer
4. If correct — chain more guesses without opening new cells

2 rounds: Round 1 — You open first. Round 2 — Computer opens first.

Scoring:

Column solved5 pts + 1 per closed cell
Final answer10 + (5 × unsolved cols) + closed cells
First guess (all closed)Up to 45 pts!
