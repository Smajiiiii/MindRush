Mind Word — Rules

Based on Moja Reč from the Serbian TV quiz show Slagalica.

How to play: 12 spinning letters are locked one by one. Build the longest possible English word within 30 seconds.

2 rounds: Round 1 — You pick letters (HOST). Round 2 — Computer picks (HOST).

Declare: When time expires, declare your word length. Mismatch = 0 points.

Scoring:

Valid wordPoints = letter count
Longer word winsOnly winner scores
Equal lengthHOST wins tie-break
Invalid / mismatch0 points
All 12 letters used+10 bonus
