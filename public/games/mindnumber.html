Mind Number — Rules

Based on Moj Broj from the Serbian TV quiz show Slagalica.

How to play: First stop 3 spinning drums to form a 3-digit target (100–999). Then stop 6 number drums (4 singles 1–9, one mid 10/15/20, one big 25/50/75/100). Use +, −, ×, ÷ and parentheses to reach the target in 30 seconds.

2 rounds: Round 1 — You pick target & numbers (HOST). Round 2 — Computer picks (HOST).

Scoring (Slagalica rules):

Closest to target wins10 pts
Tie distanceHOST wins
Loser0 pts
