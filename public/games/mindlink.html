MindLink — Rules

Based on Spojnice from the Serbian TV quiz show Slagalica.

How to play: 10 terms on the left, 10 shuffled matches on the right. Connect them in order from 1–10 by clicking the right column.

Turn taking: Active player goes in order. Correct = keep your turn and continue. Wrong = opponent gets a steal attempt on that same item.

Steal: If the opponent steals correctly, they take the turn and continue. If they miss too, neither scores that item.

2 rounds: Round 1 — You go first. Round 2 — Computer goes first.

Scoring:

Correct match2 pts
Maximum (20 pairs)40 pts
Wrong or stolen by opponent0 pts
