MindCode — Rules

Based on Skočko from the Serbian TV quiz show Slagalica.

How to play: Crack a secret 4-symbol code from 6 possible symbols. You have 6 attempts and 60 seconds per round.

Feedback pips: ● Red = correct symbol in correct position. ● Yellow = correct symbol, wrong position.

2 rounds: Round 1 — You crack Computer's code. Round 2 — Computer cracks yours.

Bonus: If a player fails all 6 attempts, opponent gets one bonus guess (10 pts if correct).

Scoring:

Solved in 1–4 attempts20 pts
Solved on 5th attempt15 pts
Solved on 6th attempt10 pts
Bonus guess correct10 pts
Failed0 pts
