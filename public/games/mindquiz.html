MindQuiz — Rules

Based on Ko Zna Zna from the Serbian TV quiz show Slagalica.

How to play: 10 trivia questions, 4 answer choices. Both you and Computer race to answer first. Clicking any answer IS the buzz — no separate button.

Faster = better. If Computer answers first, you still have ~300ms to click before the round closes.

The correct answer is always revealed after each question.

Scoring:

Correct answer+10 pts
Wrong answer−5 pts
No answer (timeout)0 pts
Both answer — faster winsSlower = 0
